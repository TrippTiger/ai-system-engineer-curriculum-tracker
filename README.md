# AI System Engineer Curriculum Tracker

An interactive web-based tracker for following a comprehensive AI Systems Engineering curriculum. Check your progress as you work through learning modules, projects, and milestones.

## 🚀 Quick Start

Open the tracker directly in your browser:
- **Live Version:** [tripptiger.github.io/ai-system-engineer-curriculum-tracker](https://tripptiger.github.io/ai-system-engineer-curriculum-tracker)

No installation or setup required—just click and start tracking your progress.

## 📚 What's Included

- **Interactive Curriculum Tracker** (`index.html`) — Check off completed modules and track your progress through the curriculum
- **Full Curriculum PDF** — A comprehensive bachelor-level curriculum covering AI systems engineering, MLOps, and related topics
- Persistent local storage of your progress (stored in your browser)

## 🎓 Curriculum Overview

The curriculum is structured around core competencies in:
- Machine Learning fundamentals and theory
- Systems engineering for ML/AI applications
- MLOps and production deployment
- Infrastructure and scalability
- And more...

See the PDF for the full breakdown of topics and learning objectives.

## 💾 How Your Progress is Saved

Your progress is automatically saved to your browser's local storage. This means:
- ✅ Your checkmarks persist between sessions
- ✅ No account or login needed
- ⚠️ Clearing browser data will reset your progress (backup if needed!)
- 🔄 Progress is device-specific (different devices = different progress tracking)

## 🛠️ Built With

- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript
- No external dependencies or frameworks
- Curriculum designed with AI assistance (Google Gemini Deep Research & Claude)
